# Chapter 27: Financial Regulatory Agencies

## Executive Summary
Overall approach to financial regulation emphasizing coordination among regulators and reversing deregulation that led to market abuses or instability.

## Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)
- Corporate accountability enforcement
- Insider trading and fraud prosecution
- ESG disclosure requirements
- Retail investor protection
- Cryptocurrency regulation

## Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)
- Strengthen CFPB authority and resources
- Predatory lending prohibition
- Student loan servicer oversight
- Debt collection abuse prevention
- Financial discrimination enforcement

## Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) and Banking Regulators
- Silicon Valley Bank lessons learned
- Adequate supervision of all banks
- Community Reinvestment Act enforcement
- Climate risk in banking
- Consumer protection in banking

**References:** Securities Exchange Act, Dodd-Frank Act, Consumer Financial Protection Act
