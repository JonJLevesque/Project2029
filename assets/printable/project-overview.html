Restorative Agenda (Ch 1-29)

Reverse all Project 2025 damage. Rebuild institutions. Restore the rule of law, civil rights, and democratic norms.

  • Eliminate Schedule F and restore civil service
  • Reinstate DOJ independence and accountability
  • Rejoin Paris Agreement, restore EPA enforcement
  • Protect voting rights, whistleblowers, inspectors general

Transformative Agenda (Ch 30+)

Go beyond restoration. Build the democracy we have never had but always promised.

  • Overturn Citizens United (30th Amendment)
  • Abolish Electoral College, Senate term limits
  • Universal Basic Services: healthcare, education, transit
  • Ranked-choice voting, publicly funded elections
