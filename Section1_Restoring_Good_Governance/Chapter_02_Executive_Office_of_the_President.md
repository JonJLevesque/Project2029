## The Project 2025 Damage Assessment

### What Project 2025 Did
- Expanded executive power beyond constitutional limits
- Undermined Office of Management and Budget (OMB) independence
- Politicized Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs (OIRA)
- Weakened Council of Economic Advisers
- Undermined National Security Council processes
- Eliminated or weakened oversight offices

### Impact on Democratic Governance
- Concentration of unchecked power
- Erosion of expert-driven policy
- Lack of transparency in decision-making
- Weakened checks and balances

## Immediate Reversal Actions (Days 1-100)

### Day One Executive Orders
1. Restore OMB and OIRA independence and integrity
2. Rebuild National Security Council processes
3. Restore oversight mechanisms
4. Implement transparency requirements

### First 100 Days Priorities
- Reconstitute advisory councils and expert bodies
- Restore scientific integrity to policy processes
- Rebuild interagency coordination mechanisms
- Implement robust ethics and transparency standards

## Democratic Renewal Agenda

### Office of Management and Budget (OMB)
**Restore Independence and Integrity**
- Evidence-based budget analysis
- Depoliticize regulatory review
- Restore professional staffing
- Implement transparency in budget processes

### Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs (OIRA)
**Rebuild Regulatory Integrity**
- Scientific integrity in regulatory review
- Transparency in cost-benefit analysis
- Public participation in regulatory process
- Balance economic efficiency with public protection

### National Security Council (NSC)
**Restore Professional Process**
- Depoliticize national security decision-making
- Restore interagency coordination
- Rebuild expert staffing
- Ensure congressional notification and oversight

### Council of Economic Advisers (CEA)
**Restore Economic Expertise**
- Professional economic analysis
- Independence from political pressure
- Transparency in economic forecasting
- Diverse economic perspectives

### Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ)
**Restore Environmental Review**
- NEPA process integrity
- Scientific basis for environmental decisions
- Public participation
- Climate change integration

### Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP)
**Rebuild Scientific Advisory Capacity**
- Restore scientific integrity policies
- Rebuild advisory committees
- Evidence-based technology policy
- Pandemic preparedness coordination
