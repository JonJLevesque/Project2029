# Contributing to Project 2029

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this policy framework.

## Ways to Contribute

- **Research:** Add citations, statistics, and legal authorities to existing chapters
- **Writing:** Expand stub chapters (see `TABLE_OF_CONTENTS.md` for status)
- **Editing:** Improve clarity, fix typos, strengthen arguments
- **Fact-checking:** Verify statute numbers, case citations, and statistics

## Before You Start

1. Read `CLAUDE.md` for project context and writing conventions
2. Review `TABLE_OF_CONTENTS.md` to understand what exists and what needs work
3. Study `Section3_The_General_Welfare/Chapter_13_Environmental_Protection_Agency.md` as the template

## Contribution Process

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch: `feature/chapter-XX-topic`
3. Follow the chapter template structure in `CLAUDE.md`
4. Ensure every policy claim cites a legal authority (statute, CFR, SCOTUS case)
5. Open a pull request with a clear description of what you added or changed

## Writing Standards

- Tone: serious, prosecutorial, evidence-based — no hedging, no passive voice
- Every claim needs a source
- No emojis in chapter content
- Use `##` for major sections, `###` for subsections
- Target 500–1,500 lines (4,000–12,000 words) per chapter

## Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
