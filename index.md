## The Agenda

### Restorative Agenda (Chapters 1-29)
- **Reverse Project 2025's harmful policies** across all federal agencies
- **Strengthen democratic institutions** and rebuild civic infrastructure
- **Establish legal accountability** for those who subverted democracy
- **Implement structural safeguards** to prevent future authoritarian threats
- **Restore American democracy** to its constitutional foundations

### Aggressive Enforcement Strategy ([Chapter 31](Section6_Safeguarding_Democracy_And_Accountability/Chapter_31_Constitutional_Hardball))
**Using Full Presidential Authority to Achieve Immediate Wins:**
- **Day 1 Executive Blitz:** Emergency declarations, IRS enforcement, FEC appointments
- **Week 1 Power Moves:** Eliminate filibuster, expand Supreme Court, DC/PR statehood
- **Month 1-3 Lock-In:** Pass democracy protection package while you have power
- **Two-Track Strategy:** Aggressive enforcement NOW + constitutional amendments for permanence

### Transformative Agenda ([Chapter 30](Section6_Safeguarding_Democracy_And_Accountability/Chapter_30_Fundamental_Democratic_Transformation))
1. **Overturn Citizens United** - End corporate money dominance
2. **Abolish Electoral College** - Direct popular vote
3. **Senate Term Limits** - 12 years maximum
4. **Publicly Funded Elections** - Democracy vouchers
5. **Supreme Court 18-Year Terms** - Predictable appointments
6. **Ranked-Choice Voting** - Break two-party stranglehold
7. **Universal Basic Services** - Healthcare, education as rights
8. **And 22 more transformative reforms...**

### Criminal Justice Transformation
*Core Principle: Justice is a public function, not a private profit center*
- **Private Prison Abolition** - Day 1 executive order ends federal contracts
- **Cash Bail Elimination** - End wealth-based pretrial detention
- **Death Penalty Abolition** - Commute all federal death sentences
- **For-Profit Healthcare Ban** - Private insurance prohibited under Medicare for All

### Technology Governance
- **AI Governance Framework** - AI Bill of Rights, algorithmic accountability
- **Puerto Rico Statehood** - 3.2 million citizens gain full voting rights
