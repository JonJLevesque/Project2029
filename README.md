# Project 2029: A Mandate for Restorative + Transformative Democratic Renewal

## Overview

Project 2029 is a comprehensive policy framework designed to counter Project 2025's authoritarian agenda and fundamentally transform American democracy. This document provides a detailed blueprint for the next democratic administration to:

### **Restorative Agenda (Chapters 1-29):**
1. **Reverse Project 2025's harmful policies** across all federal agencies
2. **Strengthen democratic institutions** and rebuild civic infrastructure
3. **Establish legal accountability** for those who subverted democracy
4. **Implement structural safeguards** to prevent future authoritarian threats
5. **Restore American democracy** to its constitutional foundations

### **Transformative Agenda (Chapter 30 + Integrated Throughout):**
6. **Overturn Citizens United** - End corporate money dominance in elections
7. **Abolish Electoral College** - One person, one vote (direct popular vote)
8. **Senate Term Limits** - End lifelong political careers (12 years maximum)
9. **Ban/Limit Lobbying** - Extended cooling-off periods, gift bans, enforcement
10. **Publicly Funded Elections** - Democracy vouchers, matching funds, level playing field
11. **Independent Congressional Ethics Body** - External enforcement with teeth
12. **Ranked-Choice Voting Nationwide** - Break two-party stranglehold
13. **Ban Micro-Targeted Political Ads** - End algorithmic psychological manipulation
14. **Universal Basic Services** - Healthcare, education, broadband, transit as rights
15. **Decentralize Federal Power** - Direct federal-city funding, regional autonomy
16. **Sunset Provisions** - Every law reviewed/renewed every 20 years
17. **Restore Fairness Doctrine** - Balanced news requirement (broadcast, cable, digital)
18. **Supreme Court 18-Year Terms** - Predictable, moderate appointments
19. **Civic Education Overhaul** - Media literacy, critical thinking, democratic values
20. **Ban Political Deepfakes** - AI transparency, mandatory labeling

**Total Scope:** 30 comprehensive chapters + Introduction + Conclusion = **Complete democratic transformation**

## Core Principles

1. **Constitutional Fidelity**: All proposals grounded in constitutional law
2. **Rule of Law**: Accountability based on legal violations, not political differences
3. **Democratic Restoration**: Rebuild institutions damaged by authoritarian governance
4. **Institutional Reform**: Strengthen checks and balances
5. **Civic Renewal**: Invest in education, civic literacy, and democratic values
6. **Due Process**: Ensure all accountability measures respect constitutional rights

## Key Mandates

### 1. Reversal of Project 2025 (Chapters 1-29)
Fully reverse Project 2025's regulatory, administrative, and ideological transformations across all federal agencies.

### 2. Strengthening Democracy (Chapters 1-29)
Rebuild democratic norms, civic infrastructure, and ensure the independence of federal agencies from political interference.

### 3. Education for Citizenship (Chapter 11 - Flagship)
Major expansion and modernization of the Department of Education to prioritize civic literacy, critical thinking, media literacy, and democratic values. $5 billion annual investment.

### 4. Justice and Ethical Governance (Chapter 17 - Expanded)
Radical reform of the Department of Justice with complete overhaul of leadership, ethics frameworks, and safeguards against political influence. Now includes campaign finance enforcement and lobbying law prosecution.

### 5. Accountability for Subversion (Chapter 28)
Implement legal accountability for individuals who participated in or enabled subversion of democracy, outlining prosecutions, applicable precedents, and safeguards to prevent recurrence.

### 6. Fundamental Democratic Transformation (Chapter 30 - NEW)
Go beyond restoration to structural transformation: overturn Citizens United, abolish Electoral College, implement publicly funded elections, ban lobbying, ranked-choice voting, universal basic services, and 6 constitutional amendments to perfect the Union.
