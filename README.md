## üéØ Overview

Project 2029 is a **414,000+ word comprehensive policy framework** designed to counter Project 2025's authoritarian agenda and fundamentally transform American democracy. It provides a detailed blueprint for the next democratic administration across **31 fully developed chapters**, **150+ proposed federal laws**, and **7 proposed constitutional amendments**.

### Two Agendas, One Mission

**Restorative Agenda** (Chapters 1-29): Reverse Project 2025's damage across every federal agency, rebuild democratic institutions, establish legal accountability, and implement structural safeguards.

**Transformative Agenda** (Chapter 30 + integrated throughout): Go beyond restoration to build the democracy we have never had but always promised -- from overturning Citizens United to universal basic services.

### üî• Aggressive Enforcement Strategy ([Chapter 31](Section6_Safeguarding_Democracy_And_Accountability/Chapter_31_Constitutional_Hardball.md))

- üî• **Day 1 Executive Blitz:** Emergency declarations, IRS enforcement, FEC appointments, DOJ priorities
- üî• **Week 1 Power Moves:** Eliminate filibuster, expand Supreme Court, DC/PR statehood
- üî• **Month 1-3 Lock-In:** Pass democracy protection package while you have power
- üî• **Two-Track Strategy:** Aggressive enforcement NOW + constitutional amendments for permanence

**Why:** Playing by rules your opponent ignores is a losing strategy. Republicans used these tactics to entrench minority rule. We use them to restore majority rule.

1. **Constitutional Fidelity** - All reforms grounded in constitutional law
2. **Rule of Law** - Accountability based on legal violations, not political differences
3. **Democratic Values** - Pluralism, tolerance, equality, rights
4. **Institutional Independence** - Agencies serve missions, not politics
5. **Evidence-Based Policy** - Facts, science, expertise guide decisions
