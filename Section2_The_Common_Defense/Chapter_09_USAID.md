# Chapter 9: U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID)

## Executive Summary

Project 2025 politicized foreign aid, undermined humanitarian missions, and prioritized political loyalty over effective development. This chapter restores USAID's mission supporting democratic institutions abroad, anti-corruption initiatives, humanitarian aid, and climate resilience.

## The Project 2025 Damage Assessment
- Humanitarian aid cuts for political reasons
- Democracy promotion programs eliminated
- Climate programs defunded
- Career development professionals purged
- Aid conditioned on authoritarian compliance

## Democratic Renewal Agenda

### Mission Restoration
**Democracy and Governance:**
- Civil society support
- Free and fair election assistance
- Anti-corruption programming
- Rule of law strengthening
- Independent media support

**Humanitarian Assistance:**
- Needs-based aid without political conditions
- Refugee and IDP support
- Disaster response capacity
- Food security programs
- Health and education access

**Climate and Development:**
- Climate adaptation assistance
- Renewable energy investment
- Sustainable development goals
- Environmental conservation
- Just transition support

### Reforms
- Restore career professional leadership
- Evidence-based programming
- Local partner empowerment
- Transparency and accountability
- Adequate resources

## Success Metrics
- Development outcomes (health, education, economic)
- Democracy indicators in partner countries
- Humanitarian needs met
- Climate resilience improvements
- Partner country satisfaction
