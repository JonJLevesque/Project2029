# Chapter 18: Department of Labor and Related Agencies

## Executive Summary
Protect workers' rights and benefits, strengthen labor standards and enforcement, empower unions, revitalize OSHA and NLRB to ensure workplace safety and fair labor practices.

## Reforms
**Worker Protection:**
- Minimum wage increase to $15+
- Overtime rule restoration
- Independent contractor misclassification enforcement
- Wage theft prosecution
- OSHA inspection and enforcement
- Workplace safety standards

**Union Rights:**
- PRO Act passage
- NLRB union election protection
- Strike and organizing rights
- Captive audience meeting prohibition
- Card check recognition
- Sectoral bargaining pilots

**References:** Fair Labor Standards Act, NLRA, OSH Act
