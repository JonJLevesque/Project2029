# Chapter 19: Department of Transportation

## Executive Summary
Modernize infrastructure with emphasis on public transit and green transportation, ensure equitable investment in communities, tighten oversight to prevent waste and political favoritism.

## Reforms
- Public transit investment ($200B)
- High-speed rail development
- EV charging infrastructure
- Bipartisan Infrastructure Law full implementation
- Transportation equity (underserved communities)
- Safety standards (rail, aviation, automotive)
- Climate mitigation in transportation

**References:** Federal-Aid Highway Act, Federal Transit Act
