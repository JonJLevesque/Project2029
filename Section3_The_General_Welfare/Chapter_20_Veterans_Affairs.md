# Chapter 20: Department of Veterans Affairs

## Executive Summary
Improve veterans' services and healthcare, increase funding for mental health and rehabilitation, streamline benefits, restore integrity and accountability in VA management.

## Reforms
- Mental health and suicide prevention ($10B increase)
- VA healthcare access expansion
- Benefits processing backlog elimination
- GI Bill expansion
- Homelessness prevention
- Toxic exposure care (burn pits, Agent Orange)
- Women veterans services
- LGBTQ+ veteran support

**References:** Veterans' Benefits Act, GI Bill
