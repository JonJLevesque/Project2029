# Chapter 14: Department of Health and Human Services

## Executive Summary
Strengthen public health infrastructure and healthcare access, depoliticize CDC and FDA, rebuild trust in health institutions, ensure pandemic preparedness guided by science and equity.

## Project 2025 Damage
- CDC and FDA politicized
- Public health guidance overridden
- Abortion access eliminated
- ACA undermined
- Medicare and Medicaid cuts
- Research censored

## Reforms
**CDC Independence:**
- Scientific leadership restoration
- Pandemic preparedness investment
- Public health infrastructure rebuilding
- State and local partnerships

**FDA Scientific Integrity:**
- Drug approval depoliticization
- Vaccine safety communication
- Food safety enforcement

**Healthcare Access:**
- ACA strengthening
- Medicaid expansion incentives
- Medicare negotiation expansion
- Reproductive healthcare protection

**References:** Public Health Service Act, Food Drug and Cosmetic Act, Affordable Care Act
