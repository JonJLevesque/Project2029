# Chapter 15: Department of Housing and Urban Development

## Executive Summary
Advance fair housing policies, expand affordable housing programs, invest in community development, reverse policies that undermined housing equity or civil rights.

## Reforms
- Fair Housing Act enforcement restoration
- Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing rule reinstatement
- Affordable housing construction ($50B investment)
- Section 8 voucher expansion
- Homelessness prevention programs
- Lead paint elimination
- Community Development Block Grant increases
- Tenant protection and rent regulation support

**References:** Fair Housing Act, Housing and Community Development Act
