# Chapter 16: Department of the Interior

## Executive Summary
Protect public lands and natural resources, honor Indigenous rights and tribal sovereignty, restore national monuments and conservation initiatives, balance resource extraction with environmental and cultural stewardship.

## Reforms
- Restore Bears Ears, Grand Staircase-Escalante, Northeast Canyons monuments
- 30x30 conservation goal (30% lands protected by 2030)
- Oil and gas leasing reform on public lands
- Tribal co-management of sacred sites
- Endangered species protection restoration
- Bureau of Land Management conservation mission
- National Park Service adequate funding
- Clean energy on public lands (solar, wind)

**References:** Antiquities Act, National Park Service Organic Act, Federal Land Policy and Management Act
