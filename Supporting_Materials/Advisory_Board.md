## Advisory Board Principles

This advisory board represents diverse perspectives united by commitment to:
- Democratic governance
- Constitutional rights
- Rule of law
- Equal justice
- Civic participation
- Institutional integrity
- Evidence-based policy

Members provide expertise, not endorsement of every proposal. Disagreements are welcome—democracy thrives on constructive debate within shared commitment to democratic values.

## Acknowledgments

Project 2029 builds on decades of work by these organizations, experts, and advocates who have defended democracy, advanced civil rights, protected the environment, fought for workers, and strengthened civic institutions.

We stand on the shoulders of giants. This document synthesizes their wisdom, experience, and commitment to the ongoing project of American democracy.
