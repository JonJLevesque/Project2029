# Key Sources and Inspirations

Project 2029 builds on decades of research, legal work, advocacy, and scholarship by the organizations and institutions listed here. This is not an advisory board — none of these organizations or individuals have a formal relationship with this project. This page acknowledges whose published work, legal filings, reports, and advocacy informed the policy proposals in these chapters.
