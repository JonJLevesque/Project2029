## âœ… THE SOLUTION

### Congressional Ethics Commission Act

**Create Independent Congressional Ethics Commission (ICEC):**

**Structure:**

**7 Commissioners:**
- Former federal judges (retired, no political ambitions)
- Cannot have served in Congress (no buddy system)
- Cannot have been political appointees in last 10 years
- Cannot be lobbyists or have lobbied in last 10 years
- Appointed by: President (2), Senate Majority (1), Senate Minority (1), House Majority (1), House Minority (1), Chief Justice (1)
- 10-year staggered terms, non-renewable
- Removable only for cause (bipartisan vote of appointers)

**Independence Guaranteed:**
- Budget: mandatory appropriation, cannot be cut by Congress
- Staff: 200+ investigators, attorneys, auditors
- Subpoena power: absolute, cannot be blocked by Congress
- Referral power: to DOJ for criminal prosecution

**Jurisdiction:**
- All members of Congress (House + Senate)
- Congressional staff (senior staff)
- Immediate family members (for financial conflicts)
- Former members (for revolving door violations)

**Powers:**

**Investigation:**
- Mandatory investigation of credible complaints (no discretion to ignore)
- Proactive investigations (doesn't need complaint)
- Financial audits (random and for-cause)
- Subpoena power for documents, testimony, financial records
- Coordinate with DOJ, FBI, IRS

**Enforcement:**
- **Fines:** up to $500,000 per violation
- **Sanctions:** loss of committee assignments, seniority, leadership positions
- **Suspension:** from voting for serious violations (pending investigation)
- **Expulsion:** recommendation to full House/Senate (requires majority vote)
- **Criminal referral:** to DOJ (bindingâ€”DOJ must investigate)
- **Public disclosure:** all findings made public (transparency)

**Rules Enforced:**

**1. Financial Conflicts of Interest:**
- Stock trading by members: **banned** (no exceptions)
- Spousal/family trading: **heavily restricted** (blind trusts required)
- Financial disclosure: comprehensive, quarterly, penalties for late filing
- Gifts: **ZERO** (no lobbyist gifts, no corporate gifts, no exceptions)

**2. Campaign Finance:**
- Coordination with super PACs: **prohibited**
- Personal use of campaign funds: **prohibited**
- Foreign donations: **prohibited** (strictly enforced)
- Dark money: **prohibited** (full transparency required)

**3. Revolving Door:**
- Members: **lifetime ban** on lobbying (see Reform #6)
- Staff: **5-year ban** on lobbying former boss
- Family members: **cannot be hired by lobbyists** during member's service

**4. Sexual Harassment:**
- Settlements: **cannot use taxpayer money** (personal liability)
- NDAs: **prohibited** (public has right to know)
- Accusations: **fully investigated** (no sweeping under rug)
- Findings: **public disclosure**

**5. Insider Trading:**
- STOCK Act enforcement: **vigorous** (currently a joke)
- Trades during classified briefings: **automatic investigation**
- Suspicious timing: **investigated and prosecuted**
- Penalties: **disgorgement of profits + fines + criminal referral**

**6. Campaign Activity:**
- Official resources for campaigns: **prohibited**
- Franking for campaign purposes: **prohibited**
- Congressional staff for campaign work: **prohibited**
- Violations: **fines + possible expulsion**

**7. Attendance and Performance:**
- Excessive absences: **fines** (prorated salary reduction)
- Refusing to vote: **documented and published**
- Failure to meet constituent obligations: **investigated**
