## ‚úÖ THE SOLUTION

### 32nd Amendment (Direct Popular Vote)

**Text:**
```
Section 1: President and Vice President elected by direct popular vote.
The person with greatest number of votes wins, provided they receive
a majority of total votes cast.

Section 2: If no person receives majority, runoff election between
top two within 60 days.

Section 3: Congress shall establish uniform national election standards.

Section 4: Takes effect for elections after December 31 following ratification.
```

### Interim Solution: National Popular Vote Interstate Compact (NPVIC)

**While amendment proceeds:**

**How NPVIC Works:**
- States agree to award electoral votes to national popular vote winner
- Takes effect when states totaling 270+ electoral votes join
- **Current status: 209 electoral votes committed (17 states + DC)**
- **Need: 61 more electoral votes**

**Federal Strategy:**
- Presidential endorsement and advocacy
- DOJ legal defense if challenged
- Target states: Minnesota (10), Virginia (13), Nevada (6), Pennsylvania (19)
- **Goal: Achieve by 2028 election**
