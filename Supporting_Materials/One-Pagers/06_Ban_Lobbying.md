**Lobbying has corrupted American democracy:**
- $3.7 BILLION spent on federal lobbying annually
- 12,000+ registered lobbyists in DC (23 for every member of Congress)

## âœ… THE SOLUTION

### Lobbying Prohibition and Transparency Act

**Part I: Ban the Revolving Door (Permanent)**

**Members of Congress:**
- **LIFETIME BAN** on becoming registered lobbyist after leaving office
- Cannot lobby former colleagues, staff, or executive branch
- Cannot be "shadow lobbyist" (unregistered but doing same work)
- Violators: 10 years prison + $1 million fine

**Cabinet Secretaries & Senior Officials:**
- **10-YEAR BAN** on lobbying after leaving office
- Applies to all Senate-confirmed positions
- Includes "shadow lobbying" and consulting for lobbying firms
- Violators: 5 years prison + $500k fine

**Congressional Staff:**
- **5-YEAR BAN** on lobbying former boss or committee
- Applies to all senior staff (chiefs of staff, legislative directors, committee staff)
- Cannot join lobbying firms even in "non-lobbying" roles (no loopholes)
- Violators: 2 years prison + $100k fine

**Why These Lengths?**
- Lifetime ban for Congress: They make the lawsâ€”should never profit from that access
- 10 years for Cabinet: By then, relationships and insider knowledge stale
- 5 years for staff: Balances fairness with preventing exploitation
- All dramatically longer than current weak 1-2 year "cooling off" periods

**Part II: Restrict Corporate Lobbying**

**Registration Requirements (Expanded):**
- ALL contacts with officials must be registered (not just "substantial" time)
- Includes "shadow lobbying" - if it looks like lobbying, it IS lobbying
- No $3,000 thresholdâ€”ALL lobbying disclosed
- Quarterly reports with details of contacts, topics, amounts paid

**Spending Limits:**
- Corporations can spend maximum $100,000/year on lobbying (down from unlimited)
- Trade associations limited to $500,000/year
- No circumventing through multiple entities
- Violators: lose lobbying privileges for 5 years + fines

**Gift Ban (Total):**
- ZERO gifts from lobbyists to officials (no exceptions)
- No meals, no travel, no campaign contributions bundled by lobbyists
- No hiring officials' family members
- Violators: 2 years prison + termination

**Foreign Agent Registration (Strengthened):**
- Foreign governments/companies CANNOT hire former US officials (ever)
- Must disclose all foreign funding sources (no shell companies)
- Violators: 5 years prison + deportation if not citizen

**Part III: Empower Public Interest**

**Public Lobbying Fund:**
- $100 million/year to fund public interest advocates
- Match corporate lobbying with environmental, consumer, labor voices
- Grants to nonprofits for lobbying in public interest
- Level the playing field (corporations vs. people)

**Congressional Access Equity:**
- Public interest groups get equal meeting time with members
- "Office hours" for constituents (vs. lobbyists only)
- Public comment periods before votes
- End "pay to play" culture
