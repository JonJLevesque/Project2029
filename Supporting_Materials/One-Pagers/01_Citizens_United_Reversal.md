### Interim Reforms (While Amendment Pending)

**Year 1 Legislation:**

1. **DISCLOSE Act (Enhanced)**
   - All spending over $10,000 disclosed within 48 hours
   - No shell companies or dark money
   - Foreign money prohibition enforced
   - Criminal penalties for violations

2. **Stop Super PACs Act**
   - Restore coordination restrictions
   - Ban corporate/union super PAC contributions
   - Limit individual super PAC donations to $10,000/year

3. **Public Company Disclosure**
   - Shareholder approval required for political spending
   - Quarterly disclosure mandatory
   - Board accountability

4. **Federal Contractor Ban**
   - No political contributions from companies with federal contracts
   - Includes executives and lobbyists
   - Contract termination for violations
