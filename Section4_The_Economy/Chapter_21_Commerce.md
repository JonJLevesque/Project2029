# Chapter 21: Department of Commerce

## Executive Summary
Foster innovation and competition while advancing labor and consumer protections, invest in technology and manufacturing, enforce antitrust measures, undo favoritism towards politically connected corporations.

## Reforms
- CHIPS Act full implementation
- Manufacturing reshoring incentives
- Small business support through EDA
- Census integrity and funding
- NOAA climate science restoration
- Patent and IP protection
- Export control (national security, not protectionism)

**References:** Commerce Department Organization Act
