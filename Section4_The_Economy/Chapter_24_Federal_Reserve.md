# Chapter 24: Federal Reserve

## Executive Summary
Preserve Federal Reserve independence while encouraging policies promoting full employment and stable prices, outline expectations for Fed transparency, diversity in leadership, coordination with fiscal policy in crises.

## Reforms
- Independence from political pressure
- Dual mandate emphasis (employment and inflation)
- Climate risk in supervision
- Payment system modernization
- Diversity in Fed leadership
- Transparency in operations

**References:** Federal Reserve Act
