# Chapter 22: Department of the Treasury

## Executive Summary
Responsible fiscal policy and financial oversight: reverse regressive tax cuts from Project 2025, ensure tax fairness, combat illicit finance and corruption, safeguard economic stability for all citizens.

## Reforms
**Tax Fairness:**
- Reverse Trump tax cuts for wealthy and corporations
- Minimum corporate tax enforcement
- Billionaire minimum tax
- Carried interest loophole closure
- Offshore tax haven crackdown

**Financial Crimes:**
- Money laundering enforcement
- Sanctions compliance
- Cryptocurrency regulation
- Tax evasion prosecution
- FATCA and CRS enforcement

**References:** Internal Revenue Code, Bank Secrecy Act
