# Chapter 25: Small Business Administration

## Executive Summary
Bolster support for small businesses and entrepreneurs through expanded access to capital, technical assistance, reducing barriers to entry, reverse policies favoring large corporations.

## Reforms
- Increased SBA lending
- Microloan program expansion
- Technical assistance and counseling
- Contracting set-asides enforcement
- Women and minority-owned business support
- Rural small business investment
- Main Street over Wall Street
