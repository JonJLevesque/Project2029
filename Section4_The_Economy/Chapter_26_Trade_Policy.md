# Chapter 26: Trade Policy

## Executive Summary
Trade strategy balancing free trade with fair trade principles, emphasize protecting workers' rights and environmental standards in trade agreements, re-engage with international trade partners through multilateral frameworks.

## Reforms
- Rejoin TPP with labor/environmental improvements (CPTPP)
- WTO reform and re-engagement
- Worker-centered trade policy
- Climate and labor standards in all agreements
- Trade Adjustment Assistance expansion
- China strategy: competition not Cold War

**References:** Trade Act of 1974, Trade Agreements Acts
